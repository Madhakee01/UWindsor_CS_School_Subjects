How important is identity in one's life?

By: Keerthana Madhavan
madhava1@uwindsor.ca 

IMAGE  |   WEALTH  |  RESPECT


The three most important ingredients for Identity is"

    Image - the good and famous persona of a person
    Wealth - the money, properties, and wealth of a person
    Respect - people's views about the person

Identity of a person is what defines his/her existence in the world. From the early years of our existence, we were consciously rooted to develop a personality or an image. So, others can recognize us. Our first step towards creating an identity is name that our parents give us when we were born. Then as we grow up and get educated, we develop an image that is based on the personal and social self. The image (or behavior) is created in the childhood years and most scientists predict it is to be permenant and not changeable. Along with the image, as we grow we get lured into the materialistic world and want to inherit rich stuff, so wealth becomes a key part of our lives. If we have an image, and wealth we are highly respected. Thus, the three most important ingredients for Identity is connected together and form a cycle and play a huge part in one's life.
